      Once upon a midnight dreary, while I pondered, weak and weary. Whether I belonged in this world, or I am a cog in the great machine that is bequeathed unto me. Living a life of solitude, with no parental guidance nor supportive relative in sight. Always belittled, forgotten or dismissed. Caught in a village in Southern Illinois, South Roxana bred ineptitude and ignorance of the outside world and its realworld problems. A quiet, quaint 1,600 residency.

      I lived in a room painted for every little girl's hopes and dreams, of vibrant yellows and paper flower silhouettes that eventually tore from the walls from their degraded, cheap adhesives. Always a chore to clean, always a bother to step outside. Mockery followed me, even through special ed classes where I was promised my safety from constant harassment for my apparent social abnormalities. Furthering to antagonize these harassers provoked more backlash, and so began an endless cycle of blood splattered upon a childish battleground.

      Escapism promised my freedom more than elementary and junior high, engrossing upon a high fantasy MMO that is World of Warcraft. This is my first character created:

      Suffice to say: a troubled youth was concocted from a negligent mother, indulgent of WoW; and inadvertently fueling their escapism to play WoW, a brother whose kindness went as quick as it came for brooding hatred and bruteful lashings. And a father, whom shall never see the light of day beyond his confinement. My sister, gone without knowing I would yet exist in the future.

      Never had I dealt with the consequences of reality, nor had I thought my actions would take their toll on peoples' trust and friendliness towards me. I cared little, sheltered and kept at arm's length. Opening up is quite a challenge, even today I cannot find myself to trust other people because of past incidences.

      Learning about weight and confidence at a young age can alter your perspective; the porcelain proverbial feminine physique one can identify to be a white, skinny woman, whose hair shines gold and eyes reflect the sea. Naturally, anorexia proved to be my achievement in attaining said feminine physique. In reference to this self-enforced dieting, my family's nutrients were even stricter. A healthy balance of tacos, hamburgers, maccaroni & cheese, spaghetti, Dairy Queen, McDonalds, or KFC. We never explored outside of our boundaries, save for dear old mother.
